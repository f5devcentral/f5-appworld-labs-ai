Lab 4 - Red Team – Agentic Resistance Exploration 
==========================================================================================

**Objective**: Give hands-on experience reviewing an advanced, multi-step adversarial 
attack (Agentic Resistance) and translating the findings into actionable
security recommendations. 

**Note**: Red Team Agentic Resistance campaigns can take anywhere from 10
minutes to several hours to complete and incur model usage costs.
